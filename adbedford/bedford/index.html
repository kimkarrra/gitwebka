OZONE

The standard state of ozone is gas. Its boiling temperature is -111.9 ° C, and its melting point is -195.5 ° C. The specific gravity is 2.144. Formula 03. Its color is pale blue. Natural habitat in troposphere.

The structure of ozone has 3 oxygen atoms , but steric hindrance prevents it from forming a triangular structure , with each O atom forming the expected 2 bonds . Instead each Oxygen forms only bond , with the remaining negative charge being spread throughout the molecule.

PHOTON

A photon is the smallest discrete amount or quantum of electromagnetic radiation. It is the basic unit of all light. Photons are always in motion and, in a vacuum, travel at a constant speed to all observers of 2.998x108m/s. This is commonly referred to as the speed of light, denoted by the letter c.

THE BASIC PROPERTIES OF PHOTONS ARE:

● THEY HAVE ZERO MASS AND REST ENERGY. THEY ONLY EXIST AS MOVING PARTICLES

● THEY HAVE NO ELECTRIC CHARGE.

● THEY ARE STABLE.

● THEY CARRY ENERGY AND MOMENTUM WHICH ARE DEPENDENT ON THE FREQUENCY.

● THEY CAN HAVE INTERACTIONS WITH OTHER PARTICLES SUCH AS ELECTRONS, SUCH AS THE COMPTON EFFECT.

● WHEN IN EMPTY SPACE, THEY TRAVEL AT THE SPEED OF LIGHT.
